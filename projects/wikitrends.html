Wikitrends - traffic trends on Wikipedia

Language
Python, shell scripting, PHP, JAVA

A bunch of scripts and programs that download and process traffic logs from Wikipedia, to find the pages with greatest uptrend in page views the last day. Extra care has been taken to optimize for low computational complexity and storage demands, and to squeeze as much performance as possible out of the available hardware.
